Their are many amazing facts for composting, these are some of my favourites:

Reduces Flood Risk

Making composting can reduce flood risks becuase increased infiltration, it prevents water from laterally flowing off the land, and potentially into storm drains and sewers

Improves Soil Structure

Compost increases the organic matter in the soil. Studies have shown this improves maximum yield potential & workability and provides better crop establishment.

Sequester Carbon

When compost is applied, it increases soil health, as microbes grow and become more plentiful. Using compost is an effective way to sequester carbon, storing it in the soil instead of releasing it into the air.

Saving Household Waste

You can save 28% of your overall household waste by composting your food scraps and yard waste.
