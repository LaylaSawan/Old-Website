Introduction to my project

Do you know globally, food loss and waste account for approximately 8-10% of greenhouse gas emissions!
My Greengeine Smart Composting System offers a cost- effective and environmentally friendly solution for producing high-quality compost.
By composting, we can reduce the negative impact of food waste on the environment by turning organic food waste into soil high in nutrition.
Based on research conducted by Live Green Toronto,
farmers and gardeners can make better decisions about their composting practices when they have access to information about the composting process.
This innovative system uses affordable microcontroller and sensors to continuously monitor critical environmental factors such as moisture, temperature, and light exposure,
to help ensuring optimal composting conditions. With the help of an IoT microcontroller,
four sensors upload key environmental data to a MySQL database in real-time.
A user-friendly web interface is provided to enable easy access to composite environmental data.
